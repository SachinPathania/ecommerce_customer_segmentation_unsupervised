event_time: Timestamp of the event.
event_type: Type of event (e.g., view, purchase).
product_id: Unique identifier of the product.
category_id: Unique identifier of the product category.
category_code: Human-readable code of the product category.
brand: Brand of the product.
price: Price of the product at the time of the event.
user_id: Unique identifier of the user.
user_session: Unique identifier of the user session.
